Momen — Privacy Policy

Momen requires no account and does not track you. Your meetings, participants, presets and statistics are stored on your device and in your own private iCloud; they are never sent to us.

To improve the app we collect anonymous product-usage analytics through PostHog (hosted in the EU). Purchases are processed by RevenueCat. We do not show ads and do not use any third-party tracking (no ATT).

Data collected: product interaction (anonymous) and purchase history — never linked to your identity and never used for tracking.
